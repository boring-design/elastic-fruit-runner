A running log of non-obvious bugs, root-cause investigations, and design decisions made while building and operating `elastic-fruit-runner`. Entries are written in the moment, dated, and biased toward the story of how we arrived at a fix — not just the fix itself. They're meant to save the next engineer (often: future us) from re-walking the same wrong paths.

If you're looking for stable, structured documentation, head to:

- [Tutorials](/tutorials/getting-started/) — task-oriented walkthroughs
- [How-to guides](/how-to/install-macos/) — recipes for specific outcomes
- [Reference](/reference/configuration/) — exhaustive specs
- [Explanation](/explanation/what-is-elastic-fruit-runner/) — concept-oriented background

Devlog posts are deliberately *not* in any of those buckets. They are time-stamped narratives that may go out of date — and that is fine. They are kept for archaeology, not for instruction.
